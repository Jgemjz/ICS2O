What are viruses and worms?

Viruses and worms are kinds of software intended to corrupt your computer. You may be wondering why you'd want to do this to someone, well in somecases it is used to get private information or to get access to someones bank information or for the pure joy of trolling.

Some ways to prevent this from happening to you are:

  1. Making sure you have a very secure password with uppercase letters and numbers
  2. Never give your passwords to anyone
  3. Always keep up with your computers updates. They may patch a way someone wants to get in.
  4. But the best way is to install a virus checker.

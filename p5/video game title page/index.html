Potato Dodge

  • My title screen will have a forest green background with a discription of what the objective for the player is ("Mr. Potato has a fear of being cooked alive. Help him avoid his fear by keeping him away from the dripping lava!") The title screen with also have a pressable button that says ("Play?")

  • The goal for the player is to avoid the falling lava for as long as you can. If you hit a lava ball, you lose one of three hearts. When you lose all hearts you lose. You also lose if the potato rots.

  • The player will control Mr. Potato with the mouse.

  • Lava will fall from the top of the canvas to the bottom (in random X positions). Once it reaches the bottom it goes to the top again. Some of the lava will fall faster than others.

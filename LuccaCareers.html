Jobs in Computer Science:

Web Designer/Programmer:

Working as a web designer basically means you'll be hired to design websites for them. They could ask for a very simple site or a extremely complex one, you must be ready for it all. This job require knowledge in computer technology and web design but with that training you get a well-paying job that pays a salary of $40 000 to $60 000 yearly.
Some companies hiring in this field are:

Baytek --- The company Baytek offers a job of all things web design and computer programming, check them out if you're interested in a job in the field of programming a web design. They're also in ottawa, so that's a plus.

Macadamian Technology --- The company Macadamian is all about pushing the boundries of technology. They make mobile apps and design websites.

How to set up WiFi

  1. When setting up WiFi (assuming you have a shared family router)
    make sure you have your computer on and your wifi password handy.
  2. Go to your computers settings and click the option to connect to a new network.
  3. Type in you password when it asks for a password.
  4. Then your computer will ask to test out your connection.
  5. If the test is successful then you should be connected.
